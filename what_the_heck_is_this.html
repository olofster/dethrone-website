Art Hack Day


What/who??
  • Art Hack Day is 24h part happening part hackathon. It's for hackers whose medium is art and artists whose medium is tech. (and organized by the same)


How ?
  • Friday evening: Socializing, beverages, pre-building. (We'll have a backlog of unbuilt art hacks to explore. Like all good ideas turns out this one has an even better predecessor)
  • Friday night: Build, build, build (+ we'll put up tents for sleeping over)
  • Saturday wee hours / day: Re-build
  • Saturday evening: Opening! (to the public)
    • (so we're on the lookout for drinks sponsors & we also love scrap materials)


Why ?!?
  • NYC startups are ripe for artistic exploitation.
  • If you can't get art done in 24h you can't get art done:-P
  • You love to flex your net art muscles
  • Ideas are free - see what happens when others work off your (art) backlog
  • Teams make better art than individuals
  • It's an anti-startup weekend.
  • There are no prototypes just finished art works
  • Art hacks don't need demos
  • Art hacks are **** even when the underlying technology no longer is.
