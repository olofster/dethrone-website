Point, Line, Surface
<

Faces Materializing on Point Line Surface

Point Line Surface is an interactive multichannel audio and video installation that allows participants to create a dynamic environment representative of how machines see the world, iteratively through scan lines. This is achieved by distorting both the video and audio using techniques such as granular synthesis and scan line filters.
