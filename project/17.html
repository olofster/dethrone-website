Pizza Machine
<
The Pizza Machine by Reed & Rader
Rader taking a photo of his piece
>

"The Pizza Machine" is a pizza based installation where, basked in the neon glow of a flashing pizza sign, you can feed slices of yummy pizza to digital people. With every press of the button, you satisfy the craving and feed one more slice. If you aren't hungry yet you will be after you watch the mock commercial of pizzas flying through the sky, being hit with bats, and shaking it.
