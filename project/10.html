Balloons
<
Laser cut head covered in latex
>
We tried to make DIY balloons with 3D printed or laser cut objects. We printed plastic geometrical shapes on the maker bot as well as assembling a 3D head out of laser cut shapes (using Autodesk's 123D Make). We covered these "balloon moulds" in liquid latex and then baked them in a make-shift oven constructed from mylar covered wine box and some incandescent lights. After the latex dries, it can be peeled off and inflated. We hoped that the balloon would retain the shape of the original 3D printed objects and yield shapes not otherwise possible through traditional manufacturing techniques.
